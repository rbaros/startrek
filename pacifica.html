Pacifica

Population: Varies depending on the season.

Climate: Fair.

Pacifica is a Class O planet located in the Taurus Reach that often is used for crewmember shore leave and diplomatic conferences, most noteably Pacifica hosted the Federation Trade Council's annual meeting in 2377, 2378, and 2379. Pacifica is renowned for its warm blue waters and fine white beaches. This planet is considered by many as a jewel of the galaxy.
