Bajor

Population: 2.3 billion

Climate: Fair...after hurricane season.

It is the 7th planet in the B'hava'el system. Bajor is known for its five moons, 26 hour days and being near the Bajoran Wormhole which provides access to the Gamma Quadrant. Take a day trip and visit the space station Deep Space Nine which orbits near the system.
